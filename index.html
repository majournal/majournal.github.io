ARCH3520 - PROFESSIONAL STUDIES


This course explains to students the role of the architectural practice in the building industry, the ethical, moral and professional obligation in architectural practice and the process and procedure for building erection in relation to various laws and regulations.

Course Learning Outcomes

By end of the course, you should be able to...

CLO1

Acquire general knowledge of the role of the architect and its relation with other professionals in the industry

CLO2

Realize the importance of ethics and moral values and obligations in architectural practice

CLO3

Understand the various laws and regulations necessary to play his role in supporting the architect in practice

Transferable Skills
Teamwork , Time management, Decision making.

Teaching-learning and assessment strategy
Lecture, discussion, assignments, test, final examination.

Mode of Delivery
Lecture and discussion.

Assessment Methods and Types
Coursework : 50%
Final Examination : 50%

COURSE OUTLINES

  • Office Procedures;
  • Authorities & Approval; roles of local authorities; submissions process and procedures.
  • Basic Project Procedures;
  • Procurement system;
  • Contract Administration
  • Project Management
